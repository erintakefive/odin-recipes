Curry

Description

This is my curry recipe. Photo is from Unsplash. I didn't make this delicious looking curry unfortunately :(

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 tin of plum tomatoes
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 400g chicken breast (approx 2) or thigh (approx 3/4)
  • Spices to taste

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook onions, garlic and spices for 5 mins
  3. Stir in tomatoes
  4. Bring water to boil and add rice
  5. Reduce to simmer for 10 minutes then take off the heat
  6. Roast or grill chicken
  7. Add to sauce and cook for 10 minutes before serving
